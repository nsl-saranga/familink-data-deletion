Familife - Data Deletion and Account Policy

Familife respects your privacy and provides clear information about how your personal data is handled.

Account Deletion

In Familife, you can request deletion of your account by contacting our support team at hashbaze@gmail.com. Alternatively, all Familife user accounts are automatically deleted within 90 days if inactive.

Data that will be deleted

  • Messages and chat history
  • Photos and media shared in Familife
  • Events created or shared in Familife
  • Profile information including names and emails

Data Retention Period

All Familife user data is permanently deleted within 90 days of account inactivity or after a deletion request. No personal data will remain after this period.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding your data or account deletion, please contact us at hashbaze@gmail.com.
